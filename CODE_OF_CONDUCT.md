# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AIGate to be a useful, welcoming open source project for developers,
maintainers, and teams improving Git workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give clear reproduction steps when reporting problems.
- Credit ideas, reports, and contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or targeted personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, repeated off-topic promotion, or bad-faith disruption.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to
temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Include links, screenshots
when appropriate, and a short description of what happened.
