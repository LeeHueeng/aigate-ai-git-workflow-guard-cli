# Governance

AIGate is currently maintainer-led.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Keep `main` releasable.
- Review pull requests with clear feedback.
- Triage issues and discussions.
- Protect user trust by prioritizing security, documentation, and stable
  release practices.
- Keep the open source CLI useful without requiring paid services.

## Decision Making

Early decisions are made by the maintainer. As the project gains contributors,
repeat contributors may be invited into scoped maintainer roles.

## Contribution Path

1. Open an issue or discussion for larger changes.
2. Submit a focused pull request.
3. Keep validation visible in the PR body.
4. Participate constructively in review.

## Commercial Boundary

The CLI core remains open source. Paid offerings may include hosted dashboards,
team reporting, managed policy packs, enterprise integrations, and private
support.
